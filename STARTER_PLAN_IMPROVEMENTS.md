# Starter Plan Improvement Suggestions

## 🎯 Quick Wins (Easy to Implement)

### 1. **Clarify Limits with Specific Numbers**
Instead of "Limited" or "Basic", use specific numbers:
- ✅ "Up to 10 product listings" → "Up to 15 product listings"
- ✅ "Limited messaging" → "Up to 5 active conversation threads"
- ✅ "Basic order management" → "Up to 10 inquiries per month"

### 2. **Add Analytics Section**
Even free users should see basic metrics:
- Product views count
- Profile visits
- Inquiry count
- Monthly summary email

### 3. **Enhanced Profile Features**
- Company logo upload (even if smaller size)
- Business description (500 characters)
- Business hours display
- Social media links (LinkedIn, Website)

### 4. **Better Communication Limits**
- "Up to 20 messages per month"
- "Email notifications for all inquiries"
- "Inquiry response templates (3 templates)"
